# Creators — app_lorenz

Figure sources for the *Physics behind the Lorenz equations* appendix
(`latexsource/app_lorenz.tex`; figures live in `latexsource/Figs_app_lorenz/`).

Neither figure is Maple-generated, so there is **no creator worksheet** — the
`.eps` files are the masters:

- `cont3d_RB_DNS.eps` — snapshot of a direct numerical simulation of
  Rayleigh–Bénard convection (simulation output; not reproducible from a
  worksheet).
- `cont3d_lorenzphysics.eps` — hand-drawn schematic of the convection-roll
  velocity/temperature field that the Lorenz variables $x,y,z$ describe.

(Both `.eps` are also used by the cont3d chapter and live in
`latexsource/Figs_cont3d/`.)
